Tree Services FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our tree services in the Antelope Valley.

What areas do you service?

We serve the entire Antelope Valley, including Palmdale, Lancaster, Quartz Hill, Rosamond, and Littlerock.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes. We have a 24/7 emergency response team for storm damage and fallen trees that pose immediate hazards.

How much does tree removal cost?

Cost varies greatly by tree size, location, and condition. We provide free, no-obligation estimates to give you an accurate price.

Is your crew insured?

Yes. LER Landscaping is a fully insured business. We take every precaution to ensure safety during potentially dangerous tree work.

Can you save a sick tree?

In many cases, yes. Our Professional Tree Care service includes health assessments and treatments designed to save trees whenever possible.

Do you haul away the wood?

Yes, all standard quotes include full cleanup and debris hauling. We can also leave wood for you if requested.
