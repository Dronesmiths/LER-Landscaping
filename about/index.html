The Founder's Story

Rooted in Reliability

LER Landscaping began with a clear mission: to provide the Antelope Valley with a complete yard service that prioritizes quality, consistency, and tree health. We saw too many properties neglected or damaged by amateur work and decided to set a higher standard.

We've grown into the region's premier choice for landscaping, irrigation, and tree care. We understand the High Desert winds and soil conditions, ensuring your yard is prepped to thrive in the elements.
